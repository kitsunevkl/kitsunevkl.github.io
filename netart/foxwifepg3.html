The snow lady (the fox child, all unknowing) was drawn beyond the limits of endurance by the demands of her demon lord husband. She wept bitterly, for what could a snow woman do to resist a demon lord with a crown of fire? Still, she prayed at night that he would never receive the prize he sought so fervently. She prayed that her children would be smart and strong enough to flee, and she prayed that one day the demon lord would be brought low by his own hubris.



The first child seemed perfect to the demon lord, with his hair of flames, but when he sought to learn to control them, his flesh burned. The demon lord tossed him aside, for what use was a demon who could not form his own flames into weapons?
