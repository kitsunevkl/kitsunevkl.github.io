Neigborhood: Staring At The Void

Projects from Art 74 class, created in spring of 2023.

The final project for this class, I decided to do a glitch art iteration on a photography project I just finished. The pictures here were taken for the project "The Flowers Are Just Flowers," which was an exploration of space and time through flower imagery.

Starting from five base photos, I added elements in photoshop, layering the elements into the existing photo though masks and selection tools. Once I had a base I was happy with, I processed the image as raw data in audacity, creating the glitched appearance.

Some of the images were further processed in photoshop by layering other images on top, dropping the opacity, and using photoshop layer filters. The end result I was going for was: going on a walk through my neighborhood, but you stared at an eldritch god a little too long right beforehand.
